01.registration.
All users need to make an account on the site to post animals in need or adopt. Personal-information will be asked upon registering process and the user and animal data will be stored in a database. If you are not a member already click here to register.
02.users/main page.
The user will be able to surf on main page viewing stray animals or post stray animals for adoption.
03.adoption.
After a user agrees to adopt a specific stray animal. Our system will match the potential new owner with the user who posted the post. From that point, Its post owners responsibility to decide if the potential owner is qualified enough to have a pet.
04.donations.
Donations will go towards food/shelter and medicine to help improve stray animals standarts of living. People who wants to donate can choose where their donation money go towards via donations page. Also people can support us via donations screen if they see fit.
